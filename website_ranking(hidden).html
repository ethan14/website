13: Kickstarter

Kickstarter seems pretty tame, but it's actually really important. Companies and products often need financing in order to get started, and they get this capital from venture capitalists: people who give startups money in order to make a profit later. Kickstarter allows people to crowdsource this kind of funding and democratizes the process, allowing new companies to get funded and giving people with less money the ability to invest in startups without risking it all.
